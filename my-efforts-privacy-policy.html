Privacy Policy for MyEfforts Chrome Extension

At MyEfforts, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This privacy policy outlines how our Chrome extension handles the information it accesses.

Data Collection and Usage

Client-Side Data Processing: Our extension only processes data within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers or third-party services.

Data Usage: The extension extracts job information and fills out forms solely for your convenience. This data is not shared or transferred outside your browser.

Local Storage

Temporary Data Storage: We may use Chrome's local storage to temporarily store extracted job information. This data is retained only as long as necessary for form-filling purposes and remains within your browser.

Data Privacy and Security

No External Transmission: We do not transmit any data outside of your device. All actions performed by the extension, including data extraction and form-filling, occur entirely on your device.

Secure Operations: Our extension does not access or modify any other data beyond the job-related information needed for its functionality.

Permissions

Webpage Access: The extension requires access to certain webpages to extract job information and fill out forms. This access is limited to when you explicitly use the extension and does not involve monitoring other sites.

Local Storage: We use Chrome's local storage solely for temporary data management and to remember your preferences.
